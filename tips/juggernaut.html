Juggernaut

Tips

  • Common knowledge, but you can activate Blade Fury while TPing and your channel won't break. Use this to go spell immune if someone is walking up to you while you're TPing or use it to show up to your destination pre-spell immune.
  • The mechanics behind right clicking during Blade Fury: Does it deal damage to heroes? No. Does it deal damage to towers? Yes. Does it apply/refresh on-hit effects like Orb of Venom or Desolator? Yes. Does it add to 'Hero attacks' for ward-type units (Nether Ward, Familiars, Serpent Wards, Supernova, Tombstone)? Yes.
  • You can Blade Fury into a Black Hole or Chronosphere without it getting canceled.
  • Rather than frantically clicking around an enemy while attempting to Blade Fury them, use a follow command (default M+left click) on the target hero. This makes you far more difficult to juke and takes the guesswork out of following the enemy. Just beware if the enemy is about to go into fog.
  • Jughead is a competent solo pusher with staying power that the Healing Ward gives creeps.
  • Healing Ward gives a fair amount of vision (500/500) and moves pretty fast this makes it a clever tool for scouting if you don't need the heal. Use it to scout Rosh or check uphill or on cliffs for you.
  • You can cast spells (except Blade Fury) and use items while mid-Omnislash (except TPs).
  • If you're 6, constantly be looking for good opportunities to Omnislash. A good way to create one is to quickly kill the enemy's creep wave and then pounce on him while he's wondering why you got four last hits and he has yet to get one. The lane push will be worth the kill.

Counters

  • If he's 6, he's constantly looking for good opportunities to Omnislash. A good way to avoid getting owned is to always stay next to your creeps and to always be a mile away from your wave when it dies.
  • Going Ethereal (from Ghost Scepter, Ethereal Blade, or Pugna's Decrepify) protects you from damage received during Omnislash. Eul's can also be used to momentarily evade Omni damage.
  • Keep tabs on Jugg's mana in lane. Blade Fury and/or Omnislash are usually free kills if Jugg has the mana to cast them. It also means that he can't escape easily if he doesn't have the mana to cast Blade Fury.
  • HOLY PEACHES, PLEASE KILL THE HEALING WARD. It takes 1 attack. Doesn't matter if it's a 1k crit from Drow or a love tap from a 10 damage Visage Familiar, KILL IT.
  • At early levels, help your teammate diffuse Omnislash damage by running in and tanking some bounces. At late levels or post-Agh, just run away because he'll probably kill you both.
  • You can Salve through a level 1 Blade Fury. The chances of being able to use a Salve without Jugg's ally taking it off or him just right clicking you at a level 1 or 2 gank are probably slim, but this is one of those tips that everyone likes to share and I want in on the action.
